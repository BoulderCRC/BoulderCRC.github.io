Pop quiz: how many of the Pac-12 universities provide ZERO paid time off for childbirth to graduate workers?

Answer: ONE. CU Boulder is the only Pac-12 university that fails to provide paid parental leave for graduate workers.

Join us TODAY, June 21st, to give University leadership a piece of our mind. We are gathering at 1:15pm on the South side of the UMC. Late arrivals might be able to meet us in UMC 235, where public comment will start as soon as 1:30pm.

Why join the CRC?

When we stand together as members of a graduate worker union to demand better working conditions, a livable wage, and comprehensive health care, we win together. With more members, we will be able to better advocate for our needs as graduate workers and push the university toward a collective bargaining agreement (CBA). Graduate unions with a CBA provide tangible outcomes for graduate workers and a CBA with graduate workers has been achieved at universities around the country.

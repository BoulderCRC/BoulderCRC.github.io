We're looking for an experienced and talented field organizer to help us build grassroots power.

Field Organizer (TEMPORARY)

The Committee on Rights and Compensation - Graduate Labor Union at the University of Colorado Boulder is looking for an experienced field organizer to help us grow our union. We are a nonprofit rank-and-file union, run by and for graduate students at CU. We aim to win a collective bargaining agreement that protects all graduate workers (teaching assistants, research assistants, graduate instructors, etc). We NEED YOU to help keep us on track!

We have funding secured for about 2 months at 8 hours per week paying $20/hour, but that can be extended depending on our success building the union together.

Task Description

  • Canvass to learn about the struggles faced by graduate workers, maintain documentation about those struggles, and distribute information about our union
  • Help collate and maintain a database of our bargaining unit and issues
  • Help “steer the ship” — keep us on track by monitoring calendar items, calling people to verify attendance at critical events, and help identify tasks that need to be completed in advance
  • Follow up on prospective members, new members, and people who have grievances
  • Bring graduate students into the CRC as dues-paying members
  • Attend biweekly organizing committee meetings
  • Send weekly field organizing report to our organizing committee

Requirements

  • Exceptional communication skills, both verbal and written
  • Very comfortable approaching and talking to strangers
  • Effective time management and a strong ability to juggle several tasks
  • Broad knowledge of, and demonstrated commitment to, anti-sexist, anti-racist, working-class struggles
  • Eagerness and a strong ability to learn about everything
  • Dedication to academic/journalistic integrity; a commitment to find out social truths and share them

Desired Experience

  • Prior active membership and/or organizing experience in a rank-and-file union strongly preferred
  • Canvassing training and experience
  • Writing and editing publication-quality rhetoric in a variety of formats (from flyers to op-eds)
  • Familiarity with higher education, especially at the University of Colorado
  • Project management
  • Familiarity working with members of the media

Compensation and Timeframe

  • Hiring ASAP
  • Approximately $20/hour contracted help (1099)
  • Duration approximately 8 hours per week for two months, pending additional funding
